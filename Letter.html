Letter of J.D. Salinger, New York, May 29, 1957

Table of contents

    1.

    Dear (Mr.)MisterKanin,

    I'm afraid what you've heard is very true - I don't see any of my fictional characters in the theater - but I'm glad you liked ZOOEY,and glad you thought and bothered to write. My stand about the theater isn't a very happy one, considering that there are so many fine people putting on plays - I'm thinking especially of Elia Kazan, whom I like very much - but I have an old and unshakable notion that I know what's best for my characters.

    Thank you, though.
    Sincerely,
    J.D. Salinger
